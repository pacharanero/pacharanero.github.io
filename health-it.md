* Founder - openhealthhub (formerly openGPSoC)
* Founder & [BDFL](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benevolent_dictator_for_life) - [Leigh Hackspace](http://www.leighhack.org) - a [Code4Health](https://code4health.org/) community
